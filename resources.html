Resources

The organizations listed below provide assistance, information and support for crime victims and their families. While we strive to keep this list current, always verify contact information and eligibility requirements with each organization directly.

Colorado

  • Colorado Division of Criminal Justice, Office for Victim Programs: dcj.colorado.gov/victim-programs
  • Colorado Crime Victim Compensation Program: Provides financial assistance for expenses related to violent crimes. Learn more
  • Victims Rights Act Complaint Form: Instructions and forms for filing a Colorado VRA complaint. File a complaint

California

  • California Victim Compensation Board (CalVCB): Provides compensation for crime-related expenses. victims.ca.gov
  • Marsy’s Law Resources: Overview of Marsy’s Law rights in California. oag.ca.gov/victimservices/marsys_law
  • California Victim Services Directory: Directory of local victim assistance centers across the state. Find help

National

  • Office for Victims of Crime (OVC): Federal agency providing resources and training for victims and service providers. ovc.ojp.gov
  • National Crime Victim Law Institute: Offers legal information and assistance for crime victims across the U.S. NCVLI
  • National Alliance of Victims’ Rights Attorneys & Advocates: Professional organization for attorneys and advocates supporting victims. navraa.org
